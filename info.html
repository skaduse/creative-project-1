Living in a van can be a very rewarding, freeing lifestyle, but it definitely comes with it's share of difficulty. Lots of people want to join the community but don't know where to start. Others have been on the road for years and are looking for ways to optimize their living or connect better with people. No matter where you're at, were here to help. The links on this page lead to websites that we believe have the best resources for van dwellers in Utah and around the globe to use to better their homes and lives. All feedback is appreciated and if theres a topic you feel should be included here, please reach out to us with the links at the bottom of the page.
